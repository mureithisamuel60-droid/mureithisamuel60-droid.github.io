Who This Site Is For

This site is for new FC Mobile players who feel confused about gameplay, wondering where to get coins, events, upgrading players, and building a strong team.

Beginner Tips

  • Do not rush to spend coins early man, you are not a kid.You are responsible, assume it's your real life money.
  • Focus on daily and weekly events.
  • Upgrade only players you plan to keep Focus on real-life situations of a player.
  • Learn basic skill moves first as a beginner, follow Jude Bellingham in the FC journey.

Common Beginner Mistakes

  • Wasting coins on weak players.
  • Ignoring events and objectives.
  • Using wrong player positions.
  • Relying too much on auto-play.
